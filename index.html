About

KLE’s College of BCA, RLS Institute welcomes you to the world of the ‘TRANSFORMERS’. Here, you get a glimpse of all the events that are excited for your arrival. Mirage has a lot of games to offer. Scourge wants to hear you defend what’s right. Stratosphere is joyous for you to explore your artistic minds. Nightbird is waiting to hand over its title. Unicorn has a hidden secret for you to find. Rhinox is on the seat and is ready to question your knowledge. Arcee seeks for the mellifluous voice which brings the melodies of a ballad back to life. Bumblebee brings out the rhythmic tunes for you to move on your tippy toes.
